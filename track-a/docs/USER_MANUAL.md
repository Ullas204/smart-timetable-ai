# User Manual

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in browser (localhost:8501 or HF Spaces URL)
2. Select your role: **Student** or **Faculty**
3. Set up your profile in the Settings tab

## Tab-by-Tab Guide

### 📊 Dashboard
- View today's events, tasks, and study stats at a glance
- Quick access to all features

### 📅 Calendar
- Visual monthly calendar with color-coded events
- Click a date to see events and tasks
- Sync with Google Calendar (one-click OAuth)

### 📋 Tasks
- Kanban board: Todo → In Progress → Completed
- Create tasks with due dates and priorities
- Drag and drop or click to update status

### 📚 Study Planning
- AI generates personalized study plans
- Based on your subjects, exams, and deadlines
- Adapts to your performance and workload

### 📝 Attendance
- Mark daily attendance per subject
- Track attendance percentage per subject
- View attendance history

### ⏱️ Focus Timer
- Pomodoro timer with customizable intervals
- Earn XP for focused study sessions
- Track focus sessions and productivity

### 🏆 Gamification
- Level up by earning XP
- Unlock achievements for milestones
- View progress and leaderboards

### 📈 Analytics
- Productivity analytics with charts
- Task completion rates and trends
- Study streak and focus time stats

### 🤖 AI Assistant
- Natural language academic assistant
- Ask anything about your schedule, tasks, or notes
- Upload documents for RAG-powered Q&A

## AI Assistant Usage

### Basic Queries
- "What's on my schedule today?"
- "Create a task: Math homework due Friday, high priority"
- "How productive was I this week?"

### RAG (Knowledge Base)
1. Go to **📚 Knowledge Base** tab
2. Upload PDF, TXT, or DOCX files
3. Ask questions in AI Assistant:
   - "What are the key topics in Chapter 3?"
   - "Summarize the notes on database normalization"
   - "Find practice questions about Python loops"

### Tool Calling Examples
- "Create a study plan for finals"
- "What are my weak subjects?"
- "Start a focus session for Physics"
- "Mark me present for Math today"

## Mobile Access
The app is responsive and works on mobile browsers via Hugging Face Spaces.
